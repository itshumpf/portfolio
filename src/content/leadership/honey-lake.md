Honey Lake was two jobs at once. The first was a construction and permitting problem — a
property that had been stripped and shut down, in front of a county that had every reason
to say no. The second was an operations problem: standing up a workforce, a safety program,
and a P&L for an event that had to work correctly the first weekend it ran, in a sport I had
to learn from the people who raced in it.

What follows is how both were done.
