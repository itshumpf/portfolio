Built a digital time-capsule product where users sealed photos and messages for future delivery,
with AI-driven photo tagging and sorting on Firebase. Sold roughly 40 ten-year capsules through
Stripe, then pivoted into QR-code group photo drops for weddings and events before deliberately
winding the product down. The lessons carried directly into the two larger launches that followed:
ship faster, design SEO-first from the start, and automate the data layer from day one.
